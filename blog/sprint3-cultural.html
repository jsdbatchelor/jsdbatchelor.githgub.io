What is Emotional Intelligence? EQ (Emotional quotient), is a scale of the ability to pick up on social queues. Then being to be able to interact with others, or a group in an effective way, while keeping their emotions professional. How is it different to IQ? IQ (Intelligence quotient) is a scale of a human brain to perform logical tasks. The higher the IQ the faster the processor, and vice versa. The difference is EQ, being aware of all your surrounds, where as IQ is how quickly your brain can solve problems. Why is it important?? EQ I how well a person can interact with a variety of different characters. When working in teams this is more important than IQ. There maybe many team members who can help you solve a problem, good EQ would ask them for help.
